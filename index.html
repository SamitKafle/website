Welcome

We are a research group focused on advancing knowledge in urban forestry through cutting-edge technologies such as Terrestrial Laser Scanning (TLS) and Near-Infrared Spectroscopy (NIRS). Our work supports sustainable urban ecosystems and arboricultural practices.

Photo Gallery

Research Areas

  • Urban forest carbon stock quantification using TLS
  • Species-specific wood density and carbon allocation
  • Combining TLS data with tree core analysis and NIRS
